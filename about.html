About Jason Wardill

Final Check is led by Jason Wardill, an experienced hospitality professional with a long-standing career in kitchens, food operations, menu development, compliance, training and new openings.

Jason has worked across a wide range of hospitality businesses, from Michelin-starred and AA rosette environments to gastro pubs, hotels, farm shops, event catering and multi-site food-led groups.

He has held senior leadership roles including Head Chef, Executive Chef, Group Executive Chef, Head of Food & Beverage, Operations Manager and Group Development Chef.

With Final Check, Jason personally delivers the audits, consultancy, support and mentoring, bringing practical hands-on experience and a commercial understanding of what makes food businesses perform well.
